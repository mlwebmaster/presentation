DATUM DPL5 PLUS LINE LASER

The Datum DPL5+ Cross Line Laser produces two bright red horizontal and vertical laser line beams for alignment work, as well as 5 spot beams for setting out 90 Degree angles quickly and easily.

With a built-in compensator, the DPL5+ quickly and accurately self levels automatically for operation with a single button and all functions easily selectable using only two buttons.

The DPL5+ is a Pulse Beam instrument that can be used externally with the Datum Pulse Laser Detector included in the kit for a working range as far as 120m.
